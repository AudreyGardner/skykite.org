SkyKite

Gather. Look up. Build a shared sky mural. Fly it in 2027.

Get the next invite What is this?

Sober-friendly · All ages · No performance

SkyKite is a simple community ritual. We meet indoors, step outside briefly to photograph the sky, then turn one image each into tiny stickers and add them to a growing communal mural.

We photograph the sky and the mural, not people. Opt out anytime.
