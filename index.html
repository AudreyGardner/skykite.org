SkyKite

We gather, slow down, and look up at the sky together. Then we turn our photos into tiny stickers and add them to a growing communal mural. That mural becomes the design for a kite flown in 2027.

Sober-friendly All ages No performance Low barrier
How it works Get updates

What happens

  1. Brief indoor welcome.
  2. 5-minute sky walk in pairs.
  3. Take a few sky photos.
  4. Pick one favorite.
  5. We print two tiny stickers: one for you, one for the mural.

Cloud Club is daytime. Star Society is after dark. Same ritual, different sky.
